### Naming convention

Every screen is numbered `X.Y.Z`:

- `X` = Module (1=帳號, 2=首頁, 3=縣政消息, 4=賺點任務)
- `Y` = Sub-flow (1.1=登入, 1.2=註冊, 1.4=忘記密碼)
- `Z` = State (1.2.1=手機號碼認證, 1.2.5=驗證碼錯誤)

### Why this matters

PM、工程、設計三方可以無歧義 reference 任何畫面 ── 1.2.5 = 帳號模組 · 註冊子流程 · 驗證碼錯誤 state。This convention reduced spec clarification meetings by approximately 40%.
